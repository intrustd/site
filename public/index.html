The intrustd model

Hardware

You run the open-source intrustd software on a certified NAS system that you install behind your network. This device is locked down and can only be accessed via WebRTC with client-side certificate verification.

Authorization

You can authorize as many devices as you’d like to access the data an your system. You can even share data with friends through secure, revokable links. Intrustd uses industry standard encryption to ensure your data is only transferred between devices you have authorized.

Connectivity

Even though your intrustd device is behind your network, you access it like any other website. Intrustd-enabled websites use WebRTC to connect directly to your device and fetch your data directly using industry-standard encryption.
